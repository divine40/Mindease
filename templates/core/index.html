About the Mindease
Mental healthcare center

The purpose of this project is to develop a web-based platform that offers mental health support to individuals dealing with psychological issues.
The platform will provide resources, self-assessment tools, and access to mental health professionals for consultations.
The goal is to make mental health services more accessible and remove stigmas associated with seeking help.
